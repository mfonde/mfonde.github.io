Hello, and Welcome!



My name is Megan Fonde. I started my career as a web developer and designer at 1150 Academy in 2019. This intensive accelerated learning program is designed specifically to launch students into careers in development.

I have always been interested in design. For several years, I have been renovating abandoned homes in Indianapolis. Downtown Indy has long been in need of some attention and TLC. Luckily, some brilliant, creative, and determined people have come out of the woodwork in the last few decades to give the city just that! I have been fortunate enough to be a part of that revolution. Creating something beautiful and functional, while keeping in mind the needs, habits, and lifestyles of the people who will be utilizing the final product, has been incredibly fun and rewarding. I am so excited to apply the skills I have developed to a new career!

Along with design, I am passionate about service. I consider customer service the keystone of everything I do professionally. I love to make things feel special. From organizing events, to developing content for marketing collateral, to managing a busy restaurant, my goal is always to produce a memorable experience for the customer.
